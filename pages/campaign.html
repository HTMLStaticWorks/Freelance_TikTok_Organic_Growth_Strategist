The Brief

Campaign Objectives

🎯

Primary Goal

Grow from 1,200 to 50,000 followers within 45 days using purely organic methods.

📊

Secondary Goal

Achieve at least 5% engagement rate and generate 1 viral video (500K+ views).

Business Goal

Build enough audience trust to launch a paid product to followers within 60 days.

Strategy

How We Cracked the Algorithm

Core Strategy Pillars

🎪

Trend-Jacking Protocol

Daily trend monitoring with 48-hour execution turnaround for maximum early-mover advantage.

🎭

3-Second Hook System

Every video opened with a curiosity-gap hook, tested against 5 variations for maximum CTR.

🔁

Watch Loop Engineering

Videos were structured to naturally loop, increasing rewatch rate and boosting algorithmic ranking.

🎵

Audio Trend Alignment

Every post used trending audio scouted 48–72 hours before it peaked on the FYP.

Execution

6-Week Content Plan

Week 1

Brand Introduction & Niche Anchoring

7 videos introducing the creator, establishing visual aesthetic. Focus: identity hooks and curiosity gaps. Result: 12K impressions.

5 trend-jacked videos + 2 original. First FYP breakthrough. Best video: 84K views. Gained 8,200 followers.

Week 3

Viral Launch & Duet Strategy

Launched strategic duet collaboration. One video hit 1.2M views. Account grew by 34K followers in 4 days.

Week 4–5

Momentum Capitalization

Rode algorithm boost with daily content aligned to new audience profile. Maintained 8–12% engagement.

Week 6

Optimization & Handoff

Final analytics review, content template handoff, and 90-day self-sustaining content calendar delivered.

Key Learnings

What This Campaign Taught Us

Duets outperform original content by 3x in early growth

Partnering with creators in adjacent niches immediately borrows their trust and audience signal.

9am and 6pm posts drove 40% more FYP distribution

Posting timing matters. Peak algorithm windows align with peak audience activity hours.

Hook A/B tested in week 1 improved watch time by 58%

Testing 5 variations of the first 3 seconds gave data to optimize every future video's opening.

Trend alignment needs to happen BEFORE the trend peaks

Posting on a trend that's already peaked results in half the reach. Timing is the differentiator.
