Hook Writing

The Hook Framework

The first 3 seconds determine everything. Use these 6 battle-tested hook patterns.

1

The Curiosity Gap

"You've been doing this wrong your entire life..." Creates an irresistible need to watch for the answer.

2

The Bold Claim

"I grew 50K followers without posting every day — here's how." Challenges existing beliefs.

3

The Pattern Interrupt

Unexpected visual or statement that breaks the scroll reflex and captures immediate attention.

4

The Transformation Hook

"Before: 0 followers. After: 83K in 45 days." The before/after creates instant engagement.

5

The Controversy Hook

"Everyone is wrong about TikTok growth — here's what nobody talks about." Drives comments.

6

The Personal Story

"The day I lost 20K followers taught me more than 5 years of studying." Story + vulnerability = retention.

Algorithm

What the Algorithm Weights

TikTok's algorithm rewards specific signals in a specific order. Understanding the weight of each signal is the key to engineering viral content.

35%

Watch Time & Retention

How long people watch and whether they rewatch the video.

25%

Shares

The most powerful engagement signal — shares signal extreme value.

20%

Comments

Comments indicate conversation-sparking content. Replies amplify this.

12%

Saves

Bookmarks indicate reference-worthy content — great for educational posts.

8%

Likes

Likes matter least — stop optimizing for likes, start optimizing for saves and shares.

Hashtag Strategy

The Cluster Hashtag Method

Stop random hashtags. Use a structured cluster approach that signals content to the right FYP audiences.

Tier 1 — Niche Tags (2)

High-specificity hashtags (under 500M views) that target your exact niche. Best for community signal.

#lifestyletiktok#outfitinspo

Tier 2 — Mid Tags (3)

500M–2B view hashtags that describe your content category. Balance between reach and relevance.

#fashion#style#ootd

Tier 3 — Broad Tags (2)

2B+ view hashtags for maximum potential reach. Use one trending hashtag from current FYP wave.

#fyp#viral
