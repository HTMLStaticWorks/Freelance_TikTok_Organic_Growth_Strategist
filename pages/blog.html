Quick Wins

10 Viral Tips You Can Use Today

01

Hook viewers with a question that creates an instant curiosity gap in your first 3 seconds.

02

Post trending audio 48–72 hours before it peaks — not after. First movers win.

03

Engineer loop endings — a video that loops gets counted as 2 views, doubling your score.

04

Reply to every comment in the first 30 minutes — it signals relevance to the algorithm.

05

Use 3-tier hashtags: 2 niche, 3 mid-range, 1 trending. Never use only broad tags.

06

Saves are the #2 strongest signal. Create "save-worthy" content: checklists, tutorials, resources.

07

Post consistently. 3–5x per week beats posting once brilliantly then going dark for 10 days.

08

Pinch the 15-second mark — add a "mini hook" to re-capture dropping viewers mid-video.

09

Your cover frame is your billboard. Make it a curiosity-inducing thumbnail even with autoplay.

10

Analyze your top 5 performing videos — they're your algorithmic fingerprint. Do more of them.
